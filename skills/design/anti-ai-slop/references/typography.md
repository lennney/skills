# Typography: Choosing Fonts With Intent

## The Rule

Pick fonts that real design studios use, not fonts that AI defaults to.

## Font Pairing Strategies

| Strategy | Display Font | Body Font | Vibe |
|----------|-------------|-----------|------|
| Serif + Sans | Cinzel, Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond | Nunito, Work Sans, Outfit | Elegant, editorial |
| Geometric + Humanist | Bebas Neue, Oswald, Anton | Inter (OK as body, never for display) | Bold, modern |
| Display + Light | DM Serif Display, Literata | DM Sans, Plus Jakarta Sans | Clean, contemporary |
| Slab + Sans | Zilla Slab, Roboto Slab | Source Sans, Open Sans | Trustworthy, stable |
| Editorial | Libre Baskerville, Lora | Source Serif, Merriweather | Long-form, reading |

## Fonts to Use (alternatives to AI defaults)

**Instead of Inter →** try Outfit, DM Sans, Plus Jakarta Sans, Nunito, Manrope
**Instead of Roboto →** try Work Sans, Source Sans, IBM Plex Sans
**Instead of Space Grotesk →** try DM Sans, Satoshi (if available), Cabinet Grotesk
**Instead of system-ui →** pick literally anything specific

## Typography Rules

- Body text: minimum 16px on mobile, 18px on desktop
- Line height: 1.5-1.75 for body, 1.1-1.3 for headings
- Max line length: 65-75 characters
- Use font-weight for hierarchy (Bold 700 for headings, Medium 500 for labels, Regular 400 for body)
- Limit to 2 fonts per page (one display, one body)
