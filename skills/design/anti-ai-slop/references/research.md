## How to Research Effectively

1. **Browse Awwwards or Godly** for 10 minutes before starting any project
2. **Save 5-10 references** that match your project's tone
3. **For each reference, note:**
   - Font pairing (use WhatFont or browser inspect)
   - Color anchor (dominant color + accent)
   - Layout structure (header → hero → sections → footer — is it symmetrical?)
   - One unique detail (a hover effect, a divider, a texture)

## Building a Reference Library

Organize by design problem, not by visual style:
- "Good hero sections" — not "dark hero sections"
- "Good card layouts" — not "blue cards"
- "Good typography" — not "serif fonts"

This helps you solve problems rather than copy aesthetics.
